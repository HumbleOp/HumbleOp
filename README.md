# HumbleOp – Debate with Purpose.

**A high-signal, no-noise arena for ideas that deserve to be heard.**

HumbleOp is a meritocratic, API-driven debate platform designed to elevate clarity, reward substance, and protect freedom of expression without chaos.

---

## Why HumbleOp?

### ✅ Clarity wins, not popularity
Only one comment per user. Every idea competes on equal ground, with community votes and structured duels deciding who takes the stage.

### 🗳️ Real-time, single-vote system
No like-spamming. One vote per user. Transparent rankings for every debate.

### ⚔️ Duel Mechanics
The most voted responder enters a timed 1-on-1 duel with the original poster. If they vanish, the runner-up steps in. You win by presence and argument.

### 🚩 Respect enforced by the community
Flag abuse, offset with likes, and auto-replace inactive winners. Moderation is logical, transparent, and community-driven.

### 🔒 Built with ethics and security
Secure auth (Argon2id), no data mining, and no surveillance capitalism. You own your words.
